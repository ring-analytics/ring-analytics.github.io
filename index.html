The Problem

There's no tool today that shows producers what may be coming, only yesterday's news.

Cattle producers time their sales against a shifting mix of market and environmental conditions, all moving at once. They've been doing this without any consolidated read that shows when those conditions actually favor selling or buying. Imagine giving producers the same decision power that Wall Street investors use when choosing the right moment to act.

01

High stakes, thin data

A single load can carry tens of thousands of dollars in swing between a good week and a bad one, with no systematic way to tell them apart in advance.

02

Barn-specific behavior

Buyer behavior varies from barn to barn in ways that aren't written down anywhere producers can use.

03

No tool built for this

General ag-market dashboards exist. Nothing is built specifically around the sell-timing decision regional feeder calf producers actually face.
